Matvii Mykhailichenko

Diagnostics Bioinformatician at the Medical University of Graz, Institute for Human Genetics.

About Me

My work focuses on developing and implementing computational solutions for genetic diagnostics and research. I utilize various bioinformatics tools to analyze complex biological data.

I am also dedicated to advancing Ukrainian bioinformatics through the Bioinformatics for Ukraine society, helping to bridge the gap between Ukrainian scientists and the international research community.
